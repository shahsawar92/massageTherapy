Health Care Coverage For Massage

Clover Therapeutic Wellness Centre offers direct billing to a number of insurance companies for massage therapy services provided for your healthcare. This service is a courtesy to our clients. We will try to direct bill to insurance companies and polices that allow for it and that will pay the healthcare provider directly. If your insurance company or policy does not allow for direct billing we will provide you a receipt for the Massage Therapy Service you have paid for and you will be able to submit it on your own for reimbursement. We accept all major credit cards, debit and cash.

If you have extended healthcare coverage for Massage Therapy through your health care plan, why not utilize them at Clover Therapeutic Wellness Centre and let us help you restore your health and well-being.

List of insurance companies that we work with are Chamber of Commerce, Cowan, Great West Life, Green Shield Canada, Industrial Alliance, Johnson Insurance, Johnston Group, Manulife Financial, Maximum Benefit, Sun Life Financial, Standard Life, First Canadian, CINUP, Blue Cross, RWAM.
